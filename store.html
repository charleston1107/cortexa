CORTEXA Store

Welcome to CORTEXA Store, the largest private hospital in Neuhumane City that only specialize in caring, treating, and repairing your livputer.

As CORTEXA gradually becomes more and more engaged in human’s lives, we are working to make the presence of this new organ as comfortable and convenient for our customers as possible. As an organ that stores data for the brain implants, livputer is easy to accumulate toxins inside and develop tissues similar to tumors as it feeds on the substance acquired from the liver. Highly concerned about people’s livputer wellbeing, we provide all kinds of medical services regarding livputers, from inspections, health consulting, cleaning operations, to data management advising.

Livputer Healthcare was founded by computer engineer Compier Prankerd and surgeon Seybert Fraudling back in 2124, when Livputer was still underdeveloped. Our experts team committed themselves to the research of providing the best livputer care with the most affordable price.

Our Mission

At Livputer Healthcare, our mission is to safeguard the health of the livputer—an essential organ at the intersection of biology and data. As livputers become deeply embedded in everyday life, we are committed to ensuring that this integration remains safe, sustainable, and humane.

We strive to make livputer care accessible, transparent, and reliable by combining advanced medical practice with cutting-edge computational research. From early inspections to long-term maintenance, our goal is to prevent datoxin accumulation, identify abnormal tissue growth, and support the stable coexistence of digital data and the human body.

Founded on collaboration between engineering and medicine, Livputer Healthcare is dedicated to continuous research, ethical care, and affordable treatment—so that every individual can live, think, and remember with confidence in their livputer’s wellbeing.
